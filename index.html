About Meisei Club

Welcome to Meisei Club (名声 = "fame") — a supportive and friendly community designed for small VTubers, PNGTubers, and oteher aspiring content creators that fall under the brand. Meisei isn’t an agency; it’s a group of peers who help each other learn, grow, and find confidence in their streaming journey.

My name is Broken Toaster, and I’m a pre-debut VTuber in training. Over the past five months, I’ve experienced the full spectrum of starting out in this world: successes, mistakes, challenges, and the occasional self-doubt. It can be especially intimidating when your inspiration and oshis are top-tier VTubers who are already integrated into their networks and meeting incredible people every day. That’s exactly why I wanted to create Meisei Club — a safe place to learn, share, and grow together.

Meisei Club provides a space where you can:

  • Receive constructive feedback and guidance on your streams.
  • Collaborate with other members on games, projects, or streams.
  • Share and discover resources, tips, and tools useful for creators.
  • Gain confidence, support, and encouragement from peers who understand the journey.

Everyone in Meisei holds a piece of the puzzle. While we aren’t here to promise instant fame or shortcuts, by working together, we can create something meaningful — a community where each member’s strengths complement the others.

Whether you’re just starting out or have been streaming for a while, Meisei Club is here to make sure your time as a member is valuable and enjoyable, no matter how long you stay.

Frequent questions and answers

I'm an upcomming Vtuber who hasn't yet made a single stream. Can I still join?

Meisei accepts creators of any size. However, it would be prefferable that you create at least 1 video/stream where your model can be seen before you join. Our security check is to prevent bots and fakes from entering, which either don't have accounts, release videos unrelated to Vtubing or have an account with no content on it. You making your first content is the only proof that you're "legit".
