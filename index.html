A creative circle for small VTubers & PNGTubers

Welcome!

Meisei Club (名声 = "reputation") is a friendly community for starting VTubers and PNGTubers who want to grow, learn, and connect with other creators.

We're not an agency — we're a group of equals helping each other find confidence, direction, and collaboration opportunities in the world of streaming.

What We Offer

  • 💬 Constructive feedback and advice on your streams
  • 🎮 Collaboration planning and community projects
  • 📚 Shared resources, tools, and tips for creators
  • ☕ A chill, respectful place to talk and grow

Join the Club

Whether you’re debuting soon or already streaming, Meisei welcomes you to find your peers, share your progress, and feel part of something real.
